OUTSTANDING CLEANING

SERVICES

01. Commercial cleaning

Clarity King provides one of the most flexible, customized cleaning services around. This service includes dusting, sweeping, vacuuming, mopping, kitchen and bathroom cleaning. We are committed to fulfilling all of our cleaning services in an environmentally friendly manner.

02. School cleaning

A clean school contributes to pleasant, healthy learning environment for children and staff. We provide full service to schools which includes day-to-day cleaning, hard floor care, carpet care, term break cleaning, window cleaning and more.

03. Healthy and Safe Cleaning

Clarity King offers effective safety training to our employees and follows a positive health and safety culture that enables us to work safely, protecting our personnel, our customers and the public. All our cleaners are covered by Public Liability insurance for absolute peace of mind.

04. Window Cleaning Services

Clarity King deliver interior window cleaning services beyond a standard window wash and wipe. You will also have the comfort to know that our window cleaning team is fully compliant with health and safety regulations and that we use eco-friendly cleaning products.

05. Carpet & Upholstery Cleaning

Clarity king has quality equipments with trained technicians for carpet cleaning. We also provide a full upholstery cleaning service. If you are looking for a professional carpet cleaning team that you can rely on to keep your carpet brilliantly clean, please give us a call.

06. Catchup clean

A Catchup clean takes care of the same tasks as a regular service but has been designed for a single visit. It is a once off service when your week gets away from you, it gives a helping hand to catch up on the weekly cleaning.
